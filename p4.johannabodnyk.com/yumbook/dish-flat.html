Herb-Crusted Roast Beef

Add to Stream

Added by

You Edit Dish

Source

The Essential James Beard Cookbook (St. Martin's Press, 2012)

Note

When in doubt, we turn to James Beard for advice on cooking large meats. The general principle of "slather with herbs, garlic, salt and pepper, and oil, then roast, holds," but he's good with the temp and minutes per pound details so you can adjust to your own roast.
